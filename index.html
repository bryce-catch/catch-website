About CATCH

Carolina Adapts Toys for Children (CATCH) is dedicated to making play accessible for every child, regardless of their abilities.

Our Story

Carolina Adapts Toys for Children began as a simple idea: what if we could modify toys to make them accessible for children with disabilities? What started as a small project at UNC Chapel Hill has grown into a thriving nonprofit organization serving families across North Carolina.

Our founders recognized that while the toy market offers countless options for typically developing children, children with disabilities often face barriers to play. Traditional toys may be difficult to activate, lack sensory feedback, or simply not be designed with accessibility in mind.

Today, we've adapted hundreds of toys, trained dozens of volunteers, and most importantly, brought smiles to countless children's faces. Our work goes beyond simple modifications ‚Äì we're building a more inclusive world where every child can participate in the fundamental human experience of play.
